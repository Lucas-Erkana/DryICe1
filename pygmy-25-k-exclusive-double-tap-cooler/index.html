PYGMY 25/K EXCLUSIVE DOUBLE TAP COOLER

PYGMY 25/K EXCLUSIVE DOUBLE TAP COOLER

The Exclusive series are a new, contemporary styled range of coolers. With a retractable handle and smooth sides, its black colour and illuminated front panel really offers a modern, sleek look. Designed for professional cooling, tapping and dispensing of excellent chilled beer both for domestic use, garden parties or functions. It comes with an inbuilt compressor.

  • New style cooler with all controls situated at the back and illuminated front panel for a more asthetic appearance
  • Includes a free stainless steel drip tray and an adjustable temperature control with 7 different settings.
  • The cooler has flow rate regulators to adjust the flow of beer from the fonts and can be set manually.
  • Continuous cooling performance is 20-25l/h. (35-45 excellent chilled pints within 1 hour)
  • The modern compressor cooling unit uses the input electric energy for direct transfer to chilling, which guarantees minimum energy usage.
  • The best chilled beer will be ready in the thermo block within 2-4 minutes after the cooler has been switched on.
  • The cooling technology is 45% more efficient than its electric energy input.
  • The complete external construction is made from stainless steel to give a more clean and aesthetic look.
  • The materials used meet the highest requirements for hygiene standards and guarantee a long working life of this cooler.
  • Pygmy coolers have been marked by expert’s findings as the beer coolers up to 25l/h. with the best ratio of price/performance/quality/dimension.
  • A retractable grab handle on top of the machine enables the machine to be carried and moved with ease.
